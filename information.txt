Mata Shitla Chaukiya dham mandir is located in the city of Jaunpur, located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Maa Sheetla is a manifestation of Goddess Shakti/Kali. It is believed that worshipping mother sheetla one gets health benefits and prosperity in life. The worship of mother goddess is going on since a very long time and a lot of people located in the Eastern Uttar Pradesh worship mother shitala with great Devotion. During the time of Navaratri the audience is at its peak.
